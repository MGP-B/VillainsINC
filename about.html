Hersheyland, the place to find inspiration.




You might be wondering, what is Hersheyland?

It all started with Milton Hershey and the classic HERSHEY’S Milk Chocolate Bar. Today, Hersheyland includes 30+ beloved brands and endless inspiration for making more moments of goodness.



HERSHEYLAND IS

the Best Candy & Snacks

From HERSHEY’S Bars and REESE’S Cups to TWIZZLERS Twists and PIRATE’S BOOTY Snacks, these beloved bites are all part of Hersheyland. Everyone’s got a favorite, what’s yours?

HERSHEYLAND IS

Made-With-Love Desserts

When iconic candy meets recipes, Hersheyland is in your kitchen, too, in every homemade creation you share with your family and friends. You can whip up birthday cupcakes, holiday cookies, ice cream sundaes, brunch desserts and any just-because lil treat you can imagine!

“The bestest chocolate cake ever!!!!”




HERSHEYLAND IS

Your Moments, Big & Small

From birthday parties and summer S’mores to sharing snacks on the couch or grabbing a treat on a road trip, Hersheyland is all about your happy moments, wherever you are.
