12 Days of Blossom Cookies

Published: Dec 15, 2023

As each holiday season inches closer, there’s something in the air that makes you want to dust off those baking pans. It’s that time of year when baking becomes more than just a culinary activity, but a cherished family tradition! That’s why we’ve gathered up 12 blossom recipes you can bake up for a touch of festive cheer that’s ready to be savored regardless of the occasion.

1. Gluten-Free Peanut Butter Blossoms

Starting off strong with our gluten-free blossom for number one because we don’t want anyone to miss out on this iconic cookie! They are just as chewy, chocolatey and packed with peanut butter flavor as the classic Peanut Butter Blossom, but this time without the flour. The best part? You can have these cookies mixed up and ready to go within 30 minutes.

See the Recipe

2. KISSES Coconut Macaroon Blossoms

Chewy macaroons serve as the base for this fun version of blossom cookies. Enjoy the delectable combination of refreshingly sweet coconut and smooth milk chocolate. Your guests will love how the coconut flakes get toasty in the oven!

Bake These Cookies

3. Triple Chocolate Blossoms

These cookies are a chocolate lover’s dream! Using HERSHEY’S Cocoa, HERSHEY’S KISSES Special Dark Candy and chocolate frosting, they are the trifecta of chocolatey goodness. Bake a batch, pour a glass of milk and prepare for the most decadent cookie experience.

Make These Cookies

4. Holiday Sugar Cookie Blossoms

A cute, yummy and simple recipe? Count us in. Whip up a simple sugar cookie mix, add seasonal HERSHEY’S KISSES Sugar Cookie Candies on top and sprinkle with decorator’s sugar for a bright and cheery cookie blossom. They’re also great easy recipe for making sweet memories with your little helpers!

Get the Recipe

5. HERSHEY'S KISSES Marbled Blossom Cookies

Gone are the days of having to decide between chocolate and vanilla - just have both! These HERSHEY’S KISSES Marbled Blossom Cookies give you the best of both worlds, including the HERSHEY’S HUGS Candy on top.

Bake These Cookies

6. Chocolate Mint Blossoms

If sophistication was a cookie, this would be the one. These cookies are made from scratch using HERSHEY’S SPECIAL DARK Cocoa and topped with the seasonal HERSHEY’S KISSES Mint Truffle Candies. This mouthwatering delight is the perfect blend of chocolatey indulgence and refreshing minty goodness.

Make These Cookies

7. KISSES Candy Cane Blossoms

Get festive this holiday season with soft, chewy cookies crowned with HERSHEY’S KISSES Candy Cane Mint Candies. Made from a classic sugar cookie base, you can dress these cookies up in different colored sprinkles to match the occasion. Bake a batch and watch as these delectable bites become the star of your celebrations!

See the Recipe

8. KISSES Red Velvet Chocolate Blossoms

A velvety cookie infused with a hint of cocoa and perfectly complemented by the sweet surprise of HERSHEY’S HUGS Candies on top. The rich, decadent flavors of red velvet combined with chocolate make these treats and absolute must-bake for anyone with a sweet tooth.

Bake These Cookies

9. KISSES Hot Cocoa Cookies

Imagine biting into a warm, chocolatey delight that captures the essence of your favorite cup of cocoa, complete with gooey HERSHEY'S KISSES Chocolates filled with Marshmallow Flavored Creme. No mug required with these cocoa cookies but still all the warmth and yumminess you’d expect.

Make These Cookies

10. The Grinch® Blossoms

It's th... th... th... The GRINCH® Blossoms. These treats are sure to make your heart grow three sizes with each bite! Using a sugar cookie mix, a few drops of food color and a bag of HERSHEY'S KISSES Milk Chocolates with Grinch® Foils, you can whip these up in no time! These Grinch® sugar cookies are easy to make and are sure to please a crowd — of humans and Whos alike.

Get the Recipe

11. Chocolate Almond Blossoms

Chocolate and almonds are the ultimate combo! The blend of flavors and textures creates a delicious treat that will make your taste buds happy from the first nibble to the last crumb. Enjoy for an afternoon pick-me-up or take to your next party to delight friends and family.

Bake These Cookies

12. KISSES Chocolate Blossom Cookies

Indulge in a chocolate lover’s dream with these KISSES Chocolate Blossom Cookies. Made with HERSHEY’S KISSES Milk Chocolates and HERSHEY’S Cocoa, each bite is a gooey, chocolatey explosion. Mix up a batch and get ready for an irresistible aroma.

Make This Cookie

Be the Talk of the Cookie Table

Blossoms are classic and a great addition to the dessert table whether it’s for the holidays, a graduation party or a picnic with friends. Bake them up for a school fundraiser or pair with some Sweet and Salty Party Snacks to be the host with the most!
