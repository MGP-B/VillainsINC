Hersheyland, the place to find inspiration.










You might be wondering,what is Hersheyland?


It all started with Milton Hershey and the classic HERSHEY’S Milk Chocolate Bar. Today, Hersheyland includes 30+ beloved brands and endless inspiration for making more moments of goodness.







HERSHEYLAND IS


the Best Candy & Snacks


From HERSHEY’S Bars and REESE’S Cups to TWIZZLERS Twists and PIRATE’S BOOTY Snacks, these beloved bites are all part of Hersheyland. Everyone’s got a favorite, what’s yours?

Made-With-Love Desserts
